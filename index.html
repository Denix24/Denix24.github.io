Capstone Project — HVALIDATE

Presenteeism Clinical Validation Dashboard

HVALIDATE is a dashboard designed to evaluate the impact of presenteeism in the workplace while preserving employee privacy. The system processes survey data, validates responses, and generates cohort-level insights that help decision-makers understand productivity loss trends.

Key Features

  • Data validation and response consistency checks
  • Severity stage metrics showing distribution across risk levels
  • Cohort-based reporting to protect individual privacy
  • Aggregated analytics outputs for leadership insights
  • Cost impact estimation of productivity loss

Privacy and Security Approach

  • Use of synthetic datasets for demonstration purposes
  • Cohort-level outputs rather than individual-level reporting
  • Minimum group size thresholds to maintain anonymity
  • Access control planning using IAM-based permissions
